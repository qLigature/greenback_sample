Who are we?

First established in 2009, Greenback emerged when its owners decided to concentrate on the aggregates industry.

Greenback's first project was a reservoir built on La Mesa by Manila Water worth over PHP 3 billion. Today, Greenback Winners Enterprise delivers top-quality construction aggregates to its numerous clients, including Manila Water and Metro Megamix.
